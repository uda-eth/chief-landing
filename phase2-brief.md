## Context
This is a 5-day go-to-market sprint for the CRS Entrepreneur in Residence role. The product is Chief, an iMessage-native AI chief of staff for founders. The landing page is already deployed at chief-mac.replit.app and will be pointed at chief.remyndai.com once DNS propagates. Webhook lead capture has already been tested and works.

## Product
**Name:** Chief
**Tagline:** Your chief of staff. In iMessage.
**One-liner:** An iMessage-native AI chief of staff that runs the admin work of your business so you stop losing billable hours to ops. Every interaction gets written to a private Obsidian vault in your iCloud.
**Surface:** iMessage primary, WhatsApp + Telegram supported
**Memory model:** Plain markdown in user's iCloud Obsidian vault
**Pricing assumption:** TBD, beta is free with 25 founder onboarding slots/month

## Primary ICP
Solo founders (pre-seed to Series A) and 2-3 person operator teams in services businesses where principal time is the bottleneck on revenue. Must be iPhone users (iMessage-native product).
