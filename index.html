01 — The problem

Why operators
stall.

Non-technical operators watch their tech-savvy peers compound productivity through AI. They want in. They open ChatGPT, get one good answer, and then nothing persists.

Failure mode 01

The tools
out-pace you.

Custom GPTs, Projects, MCP, Computer Use, Agents. By the time you've learned one, the next has shipped and the last one is buried.

Failure mode 02

Your stack
doesn't cooperate.

CRM, Gmail, calendar, Notion, Slack. None of them talk to each other on your behalf. AI lives in a separate browser tab you have to remember to open.

Failure mode 03

Admin eats
your billable hour.

For a $200/hr consultant, two hours of daily admin is $100K of foregone revenue per year. The opportunity cost compounds every quarter.

The mental model gap

Tech operators think in tools.
Non-technical operators think in teammates.
Chief is built for the second mind.

02 — How it works

Three things make
Chief different.

A chatbot answers. A teammate executes, remembers, and gets sharper. Chief is a teammate.

Pillar one

It lives in WhatsApp.

No new app. No new tab. No new login. The interface is the messaging app you already check 80 times a day. Distribution beats novelty.

  • Text it like a teammate. Voice notes welcome.
  • Forward an email or PDF — Chief reads it.
  • Mute it on weekends. It still keeps working.

05 — Honest disclaimers

Not for everyone.

A short list of who Chief is not built for, on purpose.

Engineering teams who want to build their own agents +

You're better served by Claude Code, the API, or n8n. Chief opinionates on workflow shape — that's the feature, not the bug.

Enterprises that need SOC 2, SSO, and SAML on day one +

We're sequencing those. v1 ships for solo operators and small teams (1–25). Get on the enterprise waitlist and we'll come find you.

Anyone hoping Chief replaces their CRM, ERP, or Notion +

It doesn't. Chief reads and writes to your systems of record. They stay. The drudgery of keeping them updated goes away.

Voice-first or app-first users +

v1 is text-first via WhatsApp. Voice notes are accepted as input but the surface stays text. No custom mobile app — WhatsApp is the app.
