FAQ

Questions, answered short.

Why iMessage?

Because you're already there. No new app. No new login. No friction.

What if I prefer WhatsApp or Telegram?

Both supported. iMessage is just the default for Mac and iPhone users.

Where does my data live?

In an Obsidian vault inside your personal iCloud. We don't store your knowledge base on our servers. You own it.

What if I cancel?

You keep the entire vault. Everything Chief learned about your business is yours. Forever.

How is this different from ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is a chatbot. Chief is an operator. It runs work, takes actions, and remembers everything in a knowledge base you control.
